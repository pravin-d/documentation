    Brand Portal

    Brand Portal is used to manage Brand details, manage users for Brand and create campaigns and view campaign overviews

    Brand Portal User Role

    Brand Portal has users with two different roles Admin Role - Has permissions to Create/View/Update/Delete Brand Users. - Has permissions to View/Update Brand details. - Has permissions to Create/View/Update/Delete future campaigns. - Has permissions to View/Download past campaign overviews. Employee Role - Has permissions to View Brand Users. - Has permissions to View Brand details. - Has permissions to Create/View/Update/Delete future campaigns. - Has permissions to View/Download past campaign overviews.
